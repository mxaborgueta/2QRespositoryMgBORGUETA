Horror Survival: Forsaken

Eternal Damnation

Description

Forsaken is an asymmetrical "killer vs. survival" type game where the survivor's objective is to survive until the timer runs out. The killers, however, have the objective of killing every survivor on the map, each kill granting more time for them to play around with. As of the moment, there are 5 killers and 10 survivors, all of them having special gameplay of their own. test

Survivors: Prey

Description

Survivors are the people who are DAMNED in this eternal hell. Their only objective is to survive until 4 minutes are up. However, each death adds 30 seconds onto the current timer, causing the failure of protecting your teammates being detrimental.
